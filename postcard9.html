stop the cross-fading. it never ends well.

this isn’t one of those lessons you need to learn the hard way. seriously, when you’re planning a night out or just chilling at home, keep it simple. pick one vice at a time—either enjoy that drink or light up a joint, but don’t mix the two. trust me, combining alcohol and weed can lead to some disasters, and you definitely don’t want that. so, whether you're sipping on a cocktail or taking hits from a blunt, just focus on one. your body will thank you later for not overdoing it and ruining the good times!
