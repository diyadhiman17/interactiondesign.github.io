capture EVERYTHING, you never know what will inspire you.

you think you’ll remember it all, but you won’t. the little moments, the funny stories, the details that feel unforgettable now will eventually fade. so, write it down. take photos. keep notes. even the messy, chaotic parts of life are worth remembering, because one day you’ll look back and smile at how far you’ve come. the things you think are insignificant today might end up being the memories you cherish most in the future. capture the chaos, the laughter, the tears. trust me, future-you will thank you for taking the time to hold onto these fleeting moments.
