about me

hi, i’m Diya!

i'm a graphic designer and illustrator with a passion for creating unique visuals that tell a story. i love making posters,comics and zines. over the years,i’ve developed a versatile style and a love for the creative process. i believe in the power of design to inspire and communicate, and I’m always exploring new ways to bring ideas to life.

having reached the ripe ol' age of twenty-three,i've gathered a treasure trove of valuable life lessons- mostly from recent disasters. naturally, i feel the need to document all this wisdom i've stumbled upon(and promptly forgotten).

there is just so much i want to say,which i why is simply must put everything down, before these fleeting moments of enlightenment disappear forever.
