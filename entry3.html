closure isn't always realistic.

Closure isn’t always what it seems,
A neat conclusion, sewn from dreams.
Not every end is tied with grace,
Some scars are worn, they leave no trace.

We search for answers, seek a sign,
To wrap it up, to draw a line.
But time moves on, and so do we,
Still carrying pieces we can’t see.

In the silence, the questions stay,
And part of us still feels the fray.
We try to heal, to find release,
But some wounds linger, never cease.
Not every story ends complete,
Some chapters leave us incomplete.
The truth we crave may slip away,
Yet we keep moving, day by day.

Closure’s a dream we chase, we fight,
But sometimes it’s just out of sight.
We learn to live with what remains,
In the spaces where no answer reigns.
