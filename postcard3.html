thinking of cutting your fringe? hold that thought.

you think you can do it. you can’t. trust me, i know you’ve watched a few tutorials and feel ready to take on the world with a pair of kitchen scissors, but this is not the time to experiment. leave the DIY bangs for youtube fails and let a professional handle the scissors. you’ll save yourself from a lot of regret and awkward selfies that you’ll want to hide later. your future self will look back on this moment and be so grateful you didn't go through with it.
