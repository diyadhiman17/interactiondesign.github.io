drop everything and move to new york city.

Staying where you grew up offers comfort and familiarity, a sense of safety that’s hard to leave behind. But stepping out of that comfort zone and leaving home forces you to grow in ways you can’t anticipate. You’ll discover strengths you didn’t know you had, and face challenges that will shape who you become. Trust me, putting distance between yourself and the life you’ve always known has a funny way of giving you perspective. It allows you to see the world—and yourself—with fresh eyes, unlocking new possibilities and growth that would have remained hidden in the safety of the familiar.
