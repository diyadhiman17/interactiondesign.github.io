you don't have to follow every passion to its end.

it’s okay to start things and not finish them. not every project needs to be completed, and not every passion has to turn into something productive. life isn’t a race to transform every hobby into a career or a side hustle. sometimes, it’s simply about enjoying the ride, experimenting, and letting yourself be immersed in something that brings you joy. you don’t owe anyone an outcome, and you don’t need to prove anything by finishing what you start. the value is in the experience, the growth, and the fun. give yourself permission to let go and just enjoy the process.
