learn to do things alone.

if you can’t take yourself out to dinner or catch a movie solo, you’re seriously missing out on one of life’s greatest perks: independence. i get it—it can feel a little weird at first, but spending time alone is super freeing. learn to enjoy your own company! Whether it’s treating yourself to a nice meal, hitting up that new flick, or just wandering around town, it’s all good. you don’t always need a group to have fun. go out there, enjoy yourself, and embrace the freedom of being by yourself. you might just surprise yourself!
