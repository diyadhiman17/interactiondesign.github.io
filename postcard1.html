hold your friendships close.

having a strong circle of female friends is like holding onto a life raft in a vast sea of chaos. these relationships become anchors, grounding you when the world feels overwhelming. they are the mirrors that reflect your true self, even in moments of doubt, reminding you of your worth, your strength, and your capacity to rise above any storm. through laughter, tears, and endless conversations, they offer a kind of companionship that nurtures the soul, lifts you up, and empowers you to keep going, no matter how turbulent the waters may become.
